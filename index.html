Our Objectives

Environmental Conservation

To engage in the conservation, rehabilitation, or protection of the natural environment, including flora, fauna, and biosphere.

Animal Welfare

To take care of animals, including rehabilitation and prevention of ill-treatment.

Education & Awareness

To promote education and training programs on environmental awareness and sustainable development projects.

Alternate Energy

To promote the use of alternate energy sources and reduce dependence on non-renewable resources.

Land Use Development

To improve and protect various types of land use, suitable for sustainable development as per government policies.

Land Reform Programs

To promote access to land, housing, and infrastructure, supporting official land reform programs.

National Parks

To fund the acquisition of land for national parks, dedicated reserves, and protected areas.

Water Rehabilitation

To fund the purchase of water entitlements for increasing environmental flows and restoring wetlands.

Organic Farming

To promote and support organic farming practices, including the use of bio-fertilizers and composting.

Green Fuel

To encourage the use of green fuel as an eco-friendly alternative.

Food Forest Development

To promote and develop food forests for sustainable food systems.

Indigenous Crops

To conserve and promote indigenous varieties of seeds and crops for biodiversity and food security.

Connect with Us

Stay inspired and informed by joining our growing community online. Follow us and take part in our journey towards sustainability!
